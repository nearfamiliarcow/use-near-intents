List your token on NEAR Intents and it's instantly tradeable everywhere the platform is integrated. It doesn't matter what chain your token lives on — Solana, Ethereum, Base, or anywhere else. One listing gives you access to the entire distribution network.

As the network grows, your distribution grows with it. Every new app that integrates NEAR Intents automatically inherits your token listing. You list once; your reach compounds over time.
