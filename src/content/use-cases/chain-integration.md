## What is Chain Integration?

Chain Integration adds your blockchain to the NEAR Intents network. One protocol-level integration connects your chain to 31 others — and every app on your chain inherits cross-chain swaps, deposits, and payments automatically. No per-app bridge work. No fragmented UX.

Solana did this. Within weeks of joining, ZEC, STRK, and NEAR tokens were bridged onto Solana for the first time. Every Solana app that integrates NEAR Intents gets 31-chain access without building bridge infrastructure. The same is available for your chain.
