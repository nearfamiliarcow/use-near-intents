## Logo Files (copied to `/static/logos/`)

### SVG Variants
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_Black.svg` — horizontal wordmark, black text + orange "Intents" — use on light/white backgrounds
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_White.svg` — horizontal wordmark, off-white text + orange "Intents" — use on dark backgrounds
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_Monochrome.svg` — horizontal wordmark, all black — single-color contexts
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_Stacked_Black.svg` — stacked/vertical, black text + orange
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_Stacked_White.svg` — stacked/vertical, white text + orange
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_Stacked_Monochrome.svg` — stacked, all black
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Tertiary.svg` — icon only: orange square with rounded corners + white N mark — use as favicon, app icon, small spaces

### PNG Variants (also available in 1x, 2x, 3x)
- `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Tertiary.png` — favicon fallback (1x)

## Current Usage in Site
- Header: `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_Black.svg` at `h-7`
- Footer: `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Primary_Black.svg` at `h-6`
- Favicon (SVG): `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Tertiary.svg` (set in app.html)
- Favicon (PNG fallback): `NEAR_Intents_Logo_Tertiary.png` (set in app.html)

## Brand Colors in Logos
- "NEAR" wordmark text: `#000000` (Black variant) or `#ecece9`/`#f2f2f1` (White variant)
- "Intents" wordmark text: `#fb4d01` (orange, always)
- Tertiary icon background: `#fb4d01` (orange square, rx/ry 52.66)
- Tertiary N mark: `#f2f2f1` (near-white)

## Do NOT (from brand guidelines)
- Do not recolor the logo
- Do not invert the logo
- Do not fully outline
- Do not change spacing
- Do not add effects
- Do not change the font for logo use
- Do not crop
- Do not add inner shadow
